Air

Filtration & Quality

Indoor air is, on average, several times more polluted than the air outside. Hospital-grade HEPA and carbon filtration, VOC monitoring, humidity control and biophilic ventilation, integrated invisibly into the architecture of your home.

  • Whole-home medical-grade HEPA
  • Activated carbon for VOCs and chemicals
  • Real-time air quality monitoring
  • Humidity & circulation calibration

Water

Filtration & Mineralisation

Whole-home water purification that removes chlorine, fluoride, heavy metals and microplastics — at every tap, every shower, every glass. Remineralised, structured, restored to a state your body recognises.

  • Multi-stage whole-home filtration
  • Reverse osmosis at point-of-use
  • Remineralisation & structuring
  • Shower-grade chlorine removal

Light

Circadian Architecture

Lighting is biology, not decoration. Tunable circadian lighting that mirrors the sun's progression — bright and blue-rich in the morning, warm and amber by night. Your hormones, sleep, and energy follow the light you live in.

  • Blue-light elimination after sunset
  • Daylight harvesting & glazing
  • Bedroom blackout & sunrise simulation
